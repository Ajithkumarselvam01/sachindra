About Our Company

The company was established back in 1973 and, at first, our sole aim was to provide a high standard service to the local industry. Overtime though, this modest goal began to evolve. Slowly but surely, we expanded to supply an ever-increasing range of industries; now a days, our clients produce everything from Simple O-rings to huge sophisticated multilayered rubber and metal bonded direction control Flex–seals forsatel lite launch vehicles. Moreover, the advent of the Internet meant that we can diversify once again by advertising via the World Wide Web the quality services for which we’ve come to be known. Simply put, we were forced to stop thinking locally and start thinking globally.

All of which brings us to the present day. We now operate out of a 20,000 sq. ft. factory located in the state of Kerala, Thrissur, India, which is one of the richest natural rubber producer in the world. The company is having a strategically advantageous location with three international airports and a seaport close to it. Yes, an awful lot has changed over the course of the past two decades. However at our company, we have kept some things unchanged. For instance, we have been a family-owned entity from day One, with our top goal being to always deliver a top-notch product and to do so on time, driven by our philosophy, 'Nothing is Impossible’. We know Rubber , Polyurethane & Engineering Plastics inside-out and back-to-front, and in addition to this own an in-house Lab, which ensures that whatever be your requirement, we will be able to cater to them. With an in house lathe and CNC facilities we have an added advantage of machining the mould at our presence and to fine tune it to the ultimate customer satisfaction. Our customer list includes Indian Space Research Organization, Indian Defence organizations, Oil Drilling companies, Automobile industry among other Industries both on the Public and Private sectors. When all is said and done, our reputation is our most valuable asset. It didn’t arise easily, nor was it formed overnight; rather, it came about as a result of our unwavering commitment to excellence and punctuality, and then spread throughout our client base one buyer at a time.
