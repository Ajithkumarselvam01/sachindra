Who We are

Sachindra Rubbers LLP stands as a trusted leader in high-quality, custom-moulded and extruded rubber products, delivering tailored solutions for industries around the world. Established in 1973, our company has grown from a local service provider to a renowned global supplier, meeting the demands of sectors as critical as Engineering, Defence, and Aerospace. Driven by a "Nothing is Impossible" philosophy, we blend decades of expertise with cutting-edge technology to provide our clients with everything from precision O-rings to advanced, multilayered flex-seals for satellite launch vehicles.
